Toasted Marshmallow Mocha Recipe

A rich and cozy mocha topped with homemade toasted marshmallow cream. Perfect for a campfire treat!

Submitted by: Kelvin Marron Bautista

Equipment needed:

  • Coffee maker or espresso machine
  • Saucepan
  • Whisk
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Electric mixer
  • Kitchen torch
  • Mugs

Ingredients:

  • 120ml of strong brewed coffee or espresso
  • 60ml of chocolate syrup
  • 480ml of whole milk
  • 240ml of heavy cream
  • 50g of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 large egg white
  • A pinch of cream of tartar

Method:

  1. Brew coffee or espresso and set aside.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat milk and chocolate syrup over medium heat, whisking until combined and hot.
  3. In a large bowl, whip heavy cream, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  4. In another bowl, beat egg white and cream of tartar until foamy. Gradually add powdered sugar and beat until stiff peaks form.
  5. Gently fold the egg white mixture into the whipped cream to create the marshmallow cream.
  6. Divide the hot chocolate-coffee mixture between two mugs.
  7. Top each mug with a generous dollop of marshmallow cream.
  8. Using a kitchen torch, carefully toast the marshmallow cream until golden brown.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your Toasted Marshmallow Mocha!
